Sudhir K Narula

Founder & Chief Numerologist

Sudhir K Narula is a renowned numerologist and the visionary founder of AnkJyōtish, a platform dedicated to unlocking the mysteries of numbers and their profound impact on human destiny. With decades of experience in the ancient science of numerology, Sudhir has helped countless individuals discover their true potential and navigate life's challenges through the wisdom of numbers.

As a master numerologist, Sudhir specializes in Vedic numerology, combining traditional Indian wisdom with modern insights to provide comprehensive life path analysis. His expertise spans destiny number calculations, name numerology, compatibility assessments, and personalized guidance for career, relationships, and personal growth.

Beyond his professional practice, Sudhir is passionate about sharing knowledge and empowering others. He has trained numerous students both online and offline, mentoring aspiring numerologists and enthusiasts in the art and science of numerology. His teaching methodology blends theoretical foundations with practical applications, ensuring students gain a deep understanding of numerological principles and their real-world applications.

Sudhir's commitment to excellence and his dedication to helping people achieve their highest potential have made AnkJyōtish a trusted name in numerological services. Through innovative technology and ancient wisdom, he continues to bridge the gap between traditional numerology and modern life, making this powerful knowledge accessible to everyone.

With a holistic approach that considers the interconnectedness of numbers, planets, and human destiny, Sudhir provides insights that go beyond mere calculations. His guidance helps individuals align with their true purpose, overcome obstacles, and create lives of fulfillment and success.
